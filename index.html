Gradie's Grueling Grind

Those damn adventurers! They've chased you, a defenseless Kobold, deep into this cave system and away from the rest of your tribe. Now you have to figure your way home though you feel hopelessly lost. Explore the cave and try to get back home, but careful! It feels like something is watching you...

Player Health: 100

Time: 00:00:00

Movement: WASD

Attack: Space Bar

NOTE: Gradie's sword is to heavy to walk and swing! So you'll have to stop to attack the enemies!
